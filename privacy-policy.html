Privacy Policy

CleanCopy · Last updated: July 2026

TL;DR: CleanCopy does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All processing happens 100% locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Ever.

1. Data Collection

CleanCopy does not collect any personal information, browsing data, or usage analytics. We do not use cookies, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

2. How It Works

  • Toggle — Click the extension icon to inject/remove CSS and JS that unblock copy restrictions
  • Processing — All modifications happen locally in your browser tab
  • No Network — The extension makes zero network requests

3. Permissions

CleanCopy only requests activeTab and scripting permissions — the minimum required to inject the content script when you click the toggle. It does not access your browsing history, tabs, or any other browser API.

4. Third-Party Services

CleanCopy does not connect to any third-party services, APIs, or external servers. The extension operates entirely offline.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

6. Changes

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub.
